OUR STORY

Founded by Alfred Cheong, NextGen Property believes in building a legacy through Real Estate Progression.

Quoting from an ancient book: "A good man leaves an inheritance to his children's children."

And that brings the birth of NextGen Property that firmly believes that the asset we've built so tirelessly, shouldn't just be enough for 1 generation.

This tagline "Properties For Generations" is the anchor of the entire work that NextGen Property does by sticking strongly to the principle that every singular piece of real estate we deal with should help you in building your legacy of wealth that can last for multiple generations.

Alongside, Niu Creative, a fully owned Media Agency will be the marketing arm for NextGen Property, which will be responsible for content creation and implementing social media campaigns by targeting at the right audience.

Join us in this transformative journey as we bring you the finest piece of property in Singapore. Think Real Estate; Think Generations

CORE VALUES

We're not just a realtor. We're your partner in buying, selling and renting property.

Core values are an important part of any organization. Our core values are:

Integrity

We believe that we should always do what is right for our client. The value of integrity helps us stay grounded and focus on what really counts in the end.

People Before Process

Being a real estate agency, handling property means handling people. It is important to know what our client want and need.

Transparency

Developing client trust and transparency is our top priority. We believe that transparency breeds trust. The more you know, the better decisions you can make.

Creative Marketing

We believe  creative marketing as a means to help client stand out from the competition.
